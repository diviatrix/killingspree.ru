Project Islands

A Peaceful Island Adventure

In Islands MP, you're left to explore a peaceful island after an old, rusty ship tosses you overboard. Survival is simpleâ€”you have the tools and skills to manage. The real challenge lies in the journey of discovery, building, and creating a new life in a relaxing world.

Gameplay

  • Relaxed Exploration: Move easily with smooth controls and enjoy a calm, open world where you can take things at your own pace.
  • Settle & Build: Start by finding or crafting a shelter with the tools you have. Build your home and expand as you gather resources you need.
  • Experience & Achieve: The focus isn't on difficult bosses or intense challenges, but on the joy of creating, exploring, and unlocking achievements through progress.
  • Meet & Connect: Encounter NPCs, roleplay stories, and connect with other players. Play solo or with friends on self-hosted or public server, chatting and building tiny community.
  • Grow & Explore: Expand your island, discover surrounding lands, and enjoy a cozy, retro-tech fantasy world full of wonder and calm.

This game is about taking your time, finding joy in the little things, and shaping your experience in a peaceful, rewarding way.
